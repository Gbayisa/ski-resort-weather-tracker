🏔️ Privacy Policy

Ski Resort Weather Tracker

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Ski Resort Weather Tracker ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit this website. By using the site you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or submit personal details. The site collects only the following data automatically:

  • Location coordinates — only when you use the "Use my location" browser feature. Coordinates are sent to our backend solely to find nearby ski resorts and are not stored.
  • Search inputs — city names you type in the search box are forwarded to the Open-Meteo Geocoding API for lookup. We do not log or store these queries.
  • Standard server logs — our hosting provider (Render) may collect IP addresses and request metadata as part of normal web-server operation. These are subject to Render's Privacy Policy.

3. Cookies and Advertising

This site displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to personalise ads based on your browsing history.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.
  • You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings or aboutads.info.
  • Visitors in the EU / EEA / UK / Switzerland will be shown a consent banner before personalised ads are loaded, in accordance with GDPR and the IAB Transparency and Consent Framework (TCF v2.x).

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each subject to their own privacy policies:

  • Open-Meteo — weather and geocoding data
  • OpenStreetMap — ski resort location data
  • Google AdSense — advertising
  • Render — web hosting

5. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the bottom of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in the project repository or contact the site owner directly.
